About us

Who we are

We are an American rock and pop band. After joining together in the 60s and have had numerous albums and tours in the years that have followed. We were formed in 1965 in Los Angeles, California, first for the original TV show The Monkees.

The band

Micky Dolenz (pic) Michael Nesmith (pic) Peter Tork (pic) Davy Jones (pic). As a group we have toured several times across the USA and internationally. We’ve sold more than 75 million records worldwide, which has made us one of the biggest selling groups of all time with our top-selling hits: - Last Train to Clarksville (link) - Pleasant Valley Sunday (link) - Daydream believer (link) - I’m a believer (link). Although two of our band members have passed away (Jones in 2012 and Tork in 2019), we (Dolenz and Nesmith) remain active members and continue to perform.
