About us

Who we are

We are an American rock and pop band. After joining together in the 60s, we have had numerous albums and tours in the years that have followed. We were formed in 1965 in Los Angeles, California, first for the original TV show, The Monkees. Our love for music evolved from this and we then began touring and performing across the world!

More recently we've been performing at a range of events like Weddings, Work parties, etc. If you're interested in finding out more, check out our Contact page!

The band

Micky Dolenz, Michael Nesmith, Peter Tork and Davy Jones are the members of the band. Together we have toured several times across the USA and internationally. We’ve sold more than 75 million records worldwide, which has made us one of the biggest selling groups of all time with our top-selling hits: Last Train to Clarksville, Pleasant Valley Sunday, Daydream believer, I’m a believer.
